Your Workout

3 exercises • 60 seconds • Chair & Full Body

Chair Squats

Common
🪑

Stand up and sit down using the chair for support. Focus on controlled movements and proper form.

⚠️ Safety Tips:

• Keep knees aligned with toes

• Don't let knees go past toes

• Keep back straight throughout

20 seconds × 1 set

Seated Shoulder Rolls

Fine
🦢

Sit on chair edge and roll shoulders in controlled circles. Release neck and shoulder tension.

⚠️ Safety Tips:

• Keep movements slow and controlled

• Don't force range of motion

• Stop if you feel pain

20 seconds × 1 set

Chair-Supported Calf Raises

Common
🦶

Hold chair back for balance and raise up on toes. Strengthen calves and improve ankle mobility.

⚠️ Safety Tips:

• Use chair for balance only

• Rise slowly and controlled

• Keep core engaged

20 seconds × 1 set
